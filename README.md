# Power-Bi

University projects:
1. Conclave_Report – 3 pages about the conclave that took place in 2025. The first page is a menu, the second contains general information, and the third presents the people who took part in it.
2. HBO_Report – 1 general page about films and TV series available in March 2023.
3. NBA_Report – 4 pages about the 2023/2024 NBA season. The first page is a menu, the second is about teams, the third is about players, and the fourth presents general statistics. This report also includes a tooltip that can be viewed on one of the visualizations on the statistics page.
4. TheGameAwards_Report – 1 general page about the games nominated for and awarded in 2024.
